## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the customer portal login page
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Create Account"**
3. Enter your information:
   - First and last name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Check your email for a verification link
5. Click the verification link to activate your account
6. Complete your profile setup with your service address

### Logging In

1. Go to the portal login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click **"Log In"**

Your session will remain active for your convenience, but we recommend logging out when using shared devices.

### Forgot Your Password?

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Check your inbox for a password reset link
4. Click the link (valid for 1 hour)
5. Create a new secure password
6. Log in with your new password

**Note:** If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

## Managing Invoices

### Viewing Your Invoices

1. Click **"Invoices"** in the sidebar

3. Filter by:

- **Service Date** - When service was performed
- **Services Performed** - Detailed breakdown

- **Due Date** - Payment deadline
- **Status** - Paid, Pending, or Overdue

### Paying an Invoice

**Online Payment (Recommended):**
1. Go to **"Invoices"**

3. Click **"Pay Now"**
4. Choose payment method:
   - Saved card (if you have one)

6. Review total
7. Click **"Process Payment"**
8. Receive payment confirmation and receipt

**Payment Methods Accepted:**
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Debit cards with Visa/Mastercard logo

**Payment is secure:** We use Stripe's industry-leading payment processing with bank-level encryption.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?

**Check these first:**
1. Verify email address is correct
2. Ensure password is entered correctly
3. Check Caps Lock is off
4. Try **"Forgot Password"** to reset

**Still having issues?**
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Disable browser extensions
- Try incognito/private mode

**Security locked out?**
- After 5 failed attempts, account is temporarily locked
- Wait 15 minutes and try again
- Contact support if still locked

### Payment Not Processing?

**Common issues:**
1. **Card declined:**
   - Verify card details are correct
   - Check card has sufficient funds
   - Ensure card hasn't expired
   - Contact your bank

2. **CVV error:**
   - Check 3-digit code on back of card
   - For Amex, check 4-digit code on front

3. **ZIP code mismatch:**
   - Enter billing ZIP code
   - Must match bank records

4. **Try another payment method:**
   - Use different card
   - Contact support for alternatives

### Not Receiving Notifications?

**Email notifications:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Add our email to safe senders
3. Verify email address in account settings
4. Check notification preferences are enabled

**SMS notifications:**
1. Verify phone number is correct
2. Check carrier isn't blocking messages
3. Ensure you opted in to SMS
4. Try re-saving phone number

### Page Not Loading or Errors?

1. **Refresh the page** - Click reload or press F5
2. **Clear cache:**
   - Chrome: Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data
   - Safari: Settings ‚Üí Clear History
   - Firefox: Options ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear Data
3. **Check internet connection**
4. **Try different browser**
5. **Contact support** if issue persists

### Appointment Not Showing Up?

1. **Check confirmation email** - Was confirmation sent?
2. **Look at correct date** - Check calendar month/year
3. **Verify status** - Was appointment cancelled?
4. **Refresh the page**
5. **Contact us** - We'll verify in our system
